## DELIVERY TIPS FOR WINNING

### Tone & Pacing
- **0:00-0:15**: Calm, confident. Let the brand breathe.
- **0:15-0:40**: Slightly faster. Build urgency with the problem.
- **0:40-1:05**: Matter-of-fact. You're stating facts, not attacking.
- **1:05-2:55**: Enthusiastic but controlled. This is the demo — energy matters.
- **2:55-4:30**: Slow down. Let the stats sink in. End strong.

### Key Moments to Emphasize
| Timestamp | Line | Why It Wins |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| 0:25 | "$4.5M... 287 days" | Concrete numbers create credibility |
| 0:55 | "NONE do full incident response lifecycle" | Clear differentiation |
| 2:20 | "sell this PII on the dark web" | Emotional hook — judges remember this |
| 2:30 | "DENY. 99% confidence" | Technical proof |
| 2:40 | "under 200 milliseconds" | Performance metric |
| 3:25 | "SupraWall asks... PLAYBOOK answers" | The one-liner that wins |
| 3:50 | "Your AI agents need a Judge" | Memorable close |

### Body Language (if presenting live)
- At 0:15: Step forward slightly. Problem = serious.
- At 1:05: Gesture to the screen. "Let me show you."
- At 2:15: Pause after "interesting." Let anticipation build.
- At 3:15: Open palms. Differentiation = clarity.
- At 3:50: Single nod. Confident close.

### If Judges Ask Questions
**"How is this different from a SIEM?"**
> "SIEMs log events. PLAYBOOK intercepts and enforces. The Judge Layer blocks actions before they execute. A SIEM tells you what happened. PLAYBOOK stops it from happening."

**"Why deterministic and not LLM-based?"**
> "Because LLMs can be bypassed. Context window displacement, confidence hijacking — we've proven it. Deterministic rules are immune. Zero LLM calls in the enforcement path."

**"Is this production-ready?"**
> "This IS production. Hosted on Railway. Docker Compose deployment. Caddy reverse proxy. PostgreSQL database. Resend email. Real incidents. Real verdicts."

**"What's the business model?"**
> "SaaS per-agent pricing. Enterprise tier includes custom Policy Builder with NIST ODPs. On-prem option for regulated industries."
